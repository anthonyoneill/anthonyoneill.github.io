Software Development

Oak Hamilton provides a range of software development services including mathematical modelling tools, custom software applications, data analysis software and online tools. After identifying your unique requirements Oak Hamilton plans, designs and builds bespoke software. The software produced is rigorously tested, deployed efficiently and comprehensively maintained.

In order to use algorithms on a computer it is necessary to implement them in software; writing reliable, efficient, specialised software is not an easy task. At Oak Hamilton we are able to develop software solutions to meet your needs. We deliver stable, reliable and well documented code that you can rely on. By combining existing packages and libraries with bespoke software we are able provide your business with whatever it needs.

Software is most effective when it is designed and optimised to meet a specific need. We combine our mathematical modelling and data analysis services with our software development services to ensure that your needs are met. As your needs change so does our software; we can provide ongoing support and development services to ensure that you are getting the most from your software.

Software should help you achieve what you want as easily as possible without getting in your way. We provide software that makes your work easier. Complex problems require innovative solutions so we will create software that is specifically designed for you.
