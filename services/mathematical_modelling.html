Mathematical Modelling

Oak Hamilton provides research and consultancy services for developing mathematical models of physical, engineering, computing and business systems. By constructing appropriate mathematical models systems may be analysed and optimised to improve performance. We can help your business by providing keen insight into your problems and providing robust mathematical solutions.

Applied mathematics is a bridge connecting pure mathematics with science and technology. At Oak Hamilton we use cutting edge applied mathematics to develop solutions to real world problems. Mathematical modelling is about taking a problem and developing equations that capture the essential features of the problem and so can be used to obtain quantitative or qualitative understanding of its behaviour. These models can take many forms such as differential equations, statistical models, dynamical systems or agent-based models amongst others.

The equations formulated to model a given problem are then analysed and solved, either exactly or approximately. For complex problems approximate results are usually obtained either via analytic or perturbation techniques or using numerical calculations. Developing algorithms to give accurate and efficient numerical solutions is key to the utility of any mathematical model. At Oak Hamilton we have experience in developing algorithms for a variety of mathematical problems and implementing these algorithms in software.

Mathematical models are verified to prove that they meets the specified requirements and are validated using real world data. Modelling justification, uncertainty quantification and communication with the client are taken seriously at Oak Hamilton in order to ensure that models can be utilised in an effective and appropriate way.

Applied mathematics is used throughout the modern world in seraching web pages, digital imaging, financial modelling, structural mechanics, fluid modelling, drug discovery, biological modelling, social networking and many more. Applied mathematics pervades our society because it delivers results and improves our lives. At Oak Hamilton we want mathematics to be used to improve your business and your community.
