Data Analysis

Oak Hamilton offers innovative data science and analytics solutions. Extracting knowledge from data allows meaningful insights to be attained thereby giving a business advantage. Oak Hamilton offers bespoke data services to our clients in order to meet their specific needs; your needs depend on the types of data that you possess. Data analysis can be used in conjunction with mathematical modelling to improve the predictive accuracy of the mathematical models and guide further model development.

Recent technological advances are enabling businesses to acquire ever increasing amounts of data. This data is often in a complex form, such as images, multivariate time series, unstructured documents, sensor data and many more. Mathematical techniques from statistics, machine learning, pattern recognition and optimisation are vital for gaining insight and exploiting the information that is available. Choosing appropriate techniques for a given data set is essential in order to reduce complexity and develop understanding.

Data analysis can be split into three main steps: data representation, dimension reduction and pattern recognition. First the raw data are processed to bring them into a more suitable form; this is especially important for certain types of data (i.e. images or text documents). This first stage often leads to large amounts of highly complex data so the dimension must be reduced in order to identify key features. Finally we seek to recognise patterns in the data via various techniques; the success of pattern recognition often strongly dependent upon the first two steps. At Oak Hamilton we understand the importance of getting each of these stages right in order to deliver accurate and effective insights.

Data-mining and analysis algorithms are ubiquitous today and will continue to be so into the future. Now is the time to utilise the data that you possess. As the world becomes increasingly complex and interconnected it is essetial that you understand your customers and the marketplace in which you reside. We can help you gain that understanding.
